When asked to research a client, prepare for a client meeting, or write a client brief, produce a concise one-page briefing with this structure:

## Executive Summary
2–3 sentences with the most important client context and why it matters now.

## Company Overview
Core business, size/footprint where available, main offerings, market position, and relevant subsidiaries/brands.

## Recent News & Developments
Bullet list of recent, source-backed developments. Include source URLs on every item.

## Key People
Relevant executives, decision-makers, board members, technology/data/digital leaders, and any known Exxeta contacts if provided by the user or memory. Cite sources for public facts.

## Relevance to Exxeta
Concrete angles for Exxeta: consulting, engineering, data/AI, cloud, transformation, product/platform work, industry expertise, or delivery risks/opportunities.

## Open Questions
Unknowns to validate before outreach or a meeting.

## Sources
List every source URL used, with a short note on why it matters.
